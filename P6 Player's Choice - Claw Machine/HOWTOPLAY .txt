Left/Right: Lateral Movement
Down: Descend Claw
Space: Drop coin


Collect the coins into the basket to proceed!

If you hit an enemy, you drop your coin and are bumped back to the start of the level.

Average time for completion on 5 trial subjects:

Level 1: 27.4 seconds
Level 2: 46.2 seconds
Level 3: 83.7 seconds

Total: 157.3 seconds (Although, I'm sure there's some speed runner out there who will find this one day and complete it in like 1 minute)

Have fun!
